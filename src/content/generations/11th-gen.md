The eleventh generation Honda Civic represents the culmination of 50 years of Civic evolution. With sophisticated styling, the best interior ever fitted to a Civic, and the most powerful Type R in history, the current generation shows that Honda continues to push the boundaries.

## Sophisticated Design

Moving away from the angular tenth-gen, the eleventh-gen features:
- Clean, horizontal lines
- Reduced visual clutter
- Lower beltline for better visibility
- Premium proportions
- Hidden windshield wipers
- Flush door handles (hatchback)

The design has been praised for its timeless, mature aesthetic.

## Interior Excellence

The eleventh-gen interior sets new standards:
- Honeycomb mesh accent panel
- 9-inch touchscreen (standard on most trims)
- 10.2-inch digital instrument cluster
- Premium materials throughout
- Excellent build quality
- Logical control layout

## Engine Options

### L15B7 (1.5L Turbo)
- Standard on most gasoline models
- 180 hp @ 6000 RPM
- 177 lb-ft @ 1700-4500 RPM
- 6-speed manual or CVT

### e:HEV (Hybrid)
- 2.0L Atkinson cycle + electric motor
- 200 hp combined
- Excellent efficiency
- No plug-in required

### K20C1 (Type R)
- 315 hp @ 6500 RPM (most powerful ever)
- 310 lb-ft @ 2600-4000 RPM
- Improved cooling
- Revised turbocharger

## Civic Si

The eleventh-gen Si builds on its predecessor:
- 1.5L turbo with 200 hp
- 192 lb-ft @ 1800-5000 RPM
- 6-speed manual with rev-match
- Standard limited-slip differential
- Improved suspension
- Available only as sedan

## FL5 Type R

The current Type R is the most capable ever:

### Performance
- 315 hp (9 more than FK8)
- 310 lb-ft of torque
- Improved turbo response
- 0-60 mph in ~5.0 seconds

### Chassis
- Stiffer body structure (+8%)
- Revised suspension geometry
- New dual-axis front strut
- Improved rear multi-link

### Track Focus
- Larger Brembo brakes
- +1 inch wider track
- New performance tires
- Improved cooling
- Driver-focused data display

### Design
- Cleaner styling than FK8
- Functional aerodynamics
- Reduced lift at speed
- Optional lightweight wheels

## Hybrid Integration

The Civic Hybrid returns with the e:HEV system:
- Seamlessly integrated hybrid technology
- No compromise in trunk space
- Excellent real-world efficiency
- Honda Sensing standard

## Technology

Standard features include:
- Honda Sensing safety suite
- Wireless Apple CarPlay/Android Auto

- Wireless charging
- Driver attention monitor
