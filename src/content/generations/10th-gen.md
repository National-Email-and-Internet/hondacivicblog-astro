The tenth generation Honda Civic represented a complete reinvention of Honda's iconic compact car. With turbocharged engines, aggressive styling, and—finally—the Type R for North America, the tenth gen brought Honda back to the performance forefront.

## Revolutionary Design

The tenth generation featured:
- Low, aggressive stance
- Coupe-like sedan profile
- Dramatic character lines
- Premium interior quality
- Modern technology integration

The design won multiple awards and was widely praised for its bold direction.

## Turbocharged Era

For the first time, the Civic lineup went turbo:

### L15B7 (1.5L Turbo)
- Standard on most trims
- 174-180 hp depending on configuration
- 162-177 lb-ft of torque
- Excellent fuel economy
- Strong mid-range power

### K20C1 (2.0L Turbo - Type R)
- 306 hp @ 6500 RPM
- 295 lb-ft @ 2500-4500 RPM
- VTEC Turbo technology
- Rev limit: 7000 RPM

## Civic Si

The tenth-gen Si offered:
- 1.5L turbo with 205 hp
- 192 lb-ft @ 2100-5000 RPM
- 6-speed manual with rev-match
- Adaptive dampers
- Limited-slip differential
- Coupe and sedan available

## Type R Comes to America

After decades of waiting, American enthusiasts finally got an official **Civic Type R**:

### FK8 Type R Highlights
- 306 hp K20C1 turbo
- 295 lb-ft of torque
- Brembo brakes
- 6-speed manual with rev-match
- Adaptive dampers (3 modes)
- Nürburgring lap record (FWD)

### Track Capability
The FK8 set the front-wheel-drive lap record at multiple circuits, including the Nürburgring (7:43.8).

## Limited Edition

The **Type R Limited Edition** (2020):
- 46 lbs lighter
- Deleted rear wiper, audio system, AC (option)
- Forged BBS wheels
- Phoenix Yellow exclusive color
- Only 600 units for USA

## Body Styles

- 4-door sedan
- 2-door coupe
- 5-door hatchback (including Si and Type R)

## Technology

Modern features included:
- Honda Sensing suite standard
- Apple CarPlay/Android Auto
- Turbo engine monitoring
- Adaptive cruise control
- Lane keeping assist

## Racing Success

The tenth generation dominated grassroots motorsport:
- Time Attack champions
- SCCA touring class victories
- Global touring car series
- Extensive tuner support

## Tuning Potential

The turbocharged engines responded well to modifications:
- Simple ECU tunes yielded 250+ hp (Si)
- Type R capable of 400+ hp with bolt-ons
- Strong aftermarket support

## Legacy

The tenth generation represented a new golden age for the Civic. The Type R fulfilled a decades-long promise to American enthusiasts, while the turbocharged lineup offered performance and efficiency. Multiple Car of the Year awards confirmed Honda had created something special.
