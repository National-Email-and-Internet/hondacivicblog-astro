 Lets face the facts. Lip kits are the future and big bumpers and side skirts are a thing of the past. Lip's are basically bumper extensions. Some cars come with a lip from the factory. Lips enhance styling while keeping a clean, professional look because they are mounted to your stock bumpers. Most of the time installation is quick and easy.

Now that you want a lip kit for your Honda, where do you get one? Well thats easy. You can visit a nice car part website like [ProCivic.com](http://www.procivic.com/), or you can check out the great deals on [eBay.com](http://www.ebay.com/). Most lip's come with all parts and hardware needed to install it, whichs leads us to our next question. How do I install a lip? Check out [this guide to install lip kits](http://www.hondacivicforum.com/m_293752/tm.htm).
