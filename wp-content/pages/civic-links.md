Here are some links to some cool Honda websites. This list ranges from Civic communities to blogs to informational pages. Enjoy these links and make sure to contact us if you would like to add a link to the list!

[Honda Civic Forum](http://www.hondacivicforum.com/) – Honda Civic community with thousands of members.

[SOHC Honda](http://www.sohchonda.com/) – Forum dedicated to Honda's with SOHC engines.

[Honda Tech](http://www.honda-tech.com/) – A very informational Honda forum. Lots of great articles.

[Club Civic](http://www.clubcivic.com/) – A nice Civic community with lots of friendly members.

[D Series.org](http://www.d-series.org/) – A Honda forum dedicated to Honda's with D-series engines.

[2006 JDM Civic SI FD2 Blog](http://www.hondacivic-fd2.com/) – Jack Lan's blog about his 2006 Civic SI FD2.
